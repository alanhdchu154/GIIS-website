# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 6 - Inequalities & Interval Notation

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Solve linear inequalities; graph solutions on a number line; write solutions in interval notation.
Assignment tie-in: Solve and graph 10 inequalities, including at least 3 that require reversing the inequality sign. Convert 5 graph solutions into interval notation and set-builder notation, then explain how the graph shows whether endpoints are included.
