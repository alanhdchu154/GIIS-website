# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 11 - Systems of Linear Equations

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Solve systems of equations by graphing, substitution, and elimination; interpret solutions (one, none, infinite).
Assignment tie-in: Solve 9 systems (3 by graphing, 3 by substitution, 3 by elimination). Write a word problem modeled by a system and solve it. Submit clearly labeled work for each method, identify the solution as an ordered pair, verify at least three solutions by substitution, and explain when each method is most efficient.
