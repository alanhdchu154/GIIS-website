# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 10 - Writing Linear Equations

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Write equations in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b), point-slope form, and standard form; convert between forms.
Assignment tie-in: Write the equation for 10 lines given various information (slope + point, two points, parallel/perpendicular conditions). Convert each to slope-intercept, point-slope, and standard form. For each line, show the source information, intermediate algebra, final equations, and one quick check using either a graph or a substituted point.
