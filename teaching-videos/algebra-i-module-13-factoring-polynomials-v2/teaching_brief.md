# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 13 - Factoring Polynomials

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Apply multiple factoring strategies to polynomials, including greatest common factor, trinomial factoring, difference of squares, and perfect square trinomials, then verify factors by expansion.
Assignment tie-in: Factor 15 polynomials using all learned methods. Label the strategy used for each problem, check each answer by re-expanding, and write two sentences explaining how you decided which factoring method to try first.
