# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 4 - Solving One-Step & Two-Step Equations

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Solve one-step and two-step linear equations using inverse operations, explain why each operation preserves equality, and verify every solution by substitution.
Assignment tie-in: Solve 15 equations that mix one-step, two-step, fractions, and negative coefficients. For 5 selected problems, show a verification check by substituting the solution back into the original equation, then write one original word problem modeled by a two-step equation.
