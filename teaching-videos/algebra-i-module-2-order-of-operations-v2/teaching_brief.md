# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 2 - Order of Operations

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Apply PEMDAS/BODMAS to simplify numerical and algebraic expressions; distinguish evaluation order.
Assignment tie-in: Create 5 multi-step expressions with at least 4 operations each. Solve each one step by step, label the PEMDAS rule used at every step, and write a short reflection explaining the most common mistake you had to avoid.

- title: course/module signal and parent-visible seriousness

- application: connect the idea to the course/module assignment

- path: exact Learn Portal next action
