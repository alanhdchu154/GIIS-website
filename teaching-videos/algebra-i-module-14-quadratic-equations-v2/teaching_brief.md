# Teaching Brief: Algebra I Module 14 - Quadratic Equations

Source: `server/prisma/courses/math/algebra-i.json`
Objectives: Solve quadratic equations by factoring, completing the square, and the quadratic formula; interpret discriminant.
Assignment tie-in: Solve 12 quadratic equations (4 by factoring, 4 by completing the square, 4 by quadratic formula). For each, verify using the alternative method. Identify which equations have 0, 1, or 2 real solutions using the discriminant.
